# Privacy Policy for Drive AI Renamer

**Last Updated: [Date]**

## Introduction
Drive AI Renamer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Google Workspace Add-on.

## Data We Collect
- **File Content**: When you use the add-on, we access the content of the files you explicitly select (PDFs, Images, Docs, Sheets) to generate renaming suggestions.
- **File Metadata**: We access file names and MIME types.
- **User Properties**: We store your API Key and License Key in your private Google User Properties storage.

## How We Use Your Data
- **Gemini API**: We send the file content and metadata to Google's Gemini API (Generative AI) for the sole purpose of generating new filenames based on your instructions.
- **No Storage**: We do **not** store your file content on our servers. Data is processed transiently.

## Data Sharing
- **Google**: Data is shared with Google via the Gemini API as described above.
- **Third Parties**: We do not share your data with any other third parties.

## Your Consent
By using this add-on, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy.
